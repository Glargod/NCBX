Abstract

This project implements a minimal coupled-ODE model of thalamocortical sensory gating failure in the thalamic reticular nucleus (TRN). Dopaminergic dynamics are deliberately excluded to focus on gating integrity, energy metabolism, and inflammatory processes. Monte Carlo and interactive simulations evaluate a Nature-First hybrid closed-loop intervention.
